Features

Everything you need, nothing you don't

Twelve focused toggles that put you back in control of how you experience YouTube.

🚫

Block Shorts

Completely removes Shorts shelves, reels, and navigation entries across the entire YouTube interface.

🏠

Hide Homepage Feed

Clears the recommendation grid so you visit YouTube intentionally — not because the algorithm pulled you in.

📺

Hide Sidebar

Removes related video suggestions while watching, so you finish what you came for instead of clicking into a rabbit hole.

🎬

Auto HD / 4K Quality

Enforces your chosen resolution on every video automatically — 720p, 1080p, 1440p or 4K. Works on embedded videos too.

⚡

Playback Speed Control

Set a default speed from 0.25× to 3× and have it applied automatically on every video you open.

🖼️

Block End Screens & Cards

Hides end screens, watermarks, info cards and pause overlays so nothing interrupts your viewing experience.

💬

Minimize Live Chat

Automatically collapses live chat on streams so you can focus on the content.

🙈

Blur Thumbnails

Blurs all thumbnails until you hover, reducing impulsive clicking triggered by clickbait imagery.

📊

Social Detox

Hides subscriber counts, like counts, and comments — the metrics designed to trigger comparison and anxiety.

⏸️

Pause on Load

Pauses video when you open a new tab directly to a watch page, so you consciously choose to press play.

📁

Auto Videos Tab

Redirects channel home pages straight to the Videos tab, skipping the featured/promoted content.

🐛

Report Bug

Built-in feedback button links directly to GitHub Issues or email — making it easy to report problems.

How it works

Simple to set up, runs silently

No accounts, no sign-in, no configuration files. One click and it's working.

Install the extension

Add Lucent from the Chrome Web Store with a single click. No permissions beyond YouTube are requested.

Open the popup

Click the Lucent icon in your toolbar. All blocking features are on by default — you can turn off anything you don't want.

Set your quality & speed

Pick your preferred resolution and playback speed from the dropdowns. They'll apply automatically on every video.

Browse YouTube your way

Settings save instantly and sync across your signed-in Chrome devices. YouTube stays distraction-free everywhere.

Privacy

Zero data collected. Full stop.

Lucent has no backend, no analytics, no telemetry. Everything stays on your device.

✓

No personal data collected

Your browsing history, watch history, and identity are never accessed or stored.

✓

No remote servers

The extension never makes network requests to any server. It only communicates with YouTube's own page.

✓

No analytics or tracking

No usage statistics, crash reports, or telemetry of any kind are ever collected.

✓

Settings stay local

Your preferences are stored using chrome.storage.sync — synced only across your own signed-in Chrome devices.

✓

YouTube only

Lucent only activates on youtube.com and youtube-nocookie.com. It has no access to any other website.

✓

Open source

Every line of code is publicly available on GitHub. You can verify exactly what the extension does.
